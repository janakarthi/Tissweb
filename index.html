Our History

 
  • Initaialed VITHIGAL -“An Inter school competition for special children” In Anna University,Chennai.
  • Initiated Adopt a Village school program- far giving regular educational support and career guidance(Village name: Koovathur on outskirts of Chennai)

Our Objective

 
  • Montessori way of education
  • CBSE syllabus
  • Individual talent identification & developing those.
  • Individual no. of students per class -Max 30/class
  • Holistic education - scientific, environmental, cultural and moral education

Why this initiative?

 
  • In Tenkasi, 70% of the population depend on agriculture for whom accessibility and affordability of quality education is a distant dream
  • Even the talented wards of poor farmers are not ablt to excel in their career
  • We aim to bridge the gap between school education and career oppurtunities structurally
  • We intend to create thin UNIVERSAL SCHOOL as a model school which could be replicated anywhere

About

Our Vision:

“ If the poor boy cannot come to education, education must go to him”
- Swami Vivekananda
 
  • Establishment of Universal School with accessible education to all children

  • Career focused education with life science education

Our mission

  • Development of basic infrastructure like Play area, RO facility, interiors for classrooms
  • Providing free evening tution to secondary school students from government schools
